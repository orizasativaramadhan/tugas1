Kevin Sanjaya Sukamuljo

Who is Kevin?

Kevin Sanjaya Sukamuljo is one of Indonesia’s top men’s doubles badminton players, born in Banyuwangi on August 2, 1995. He is best known as the regular partner of Marcus Fernaldi Gideon in the men’s doubles category and is nicknamed "The Minions" due to their short stature yet fast and dynamic playing style. Kevin joined PB Djarum in 2007 and has shown outstanding talent from a young age, with a playing style characterized by speed, aggression, and sharp reflexes, especially at the front of the court. Together with Marcus, Kevin has achieved numerous prestigious titles, including the All England Championships in 2017 and 2018, and held the world No. 1 ranking for over 100 consecutive weeks. They have also won dozens of titles on the BWF World Tour and Super Series. In addition to his technical skills, Kevin is known for his humble and humorous personality and has a strong fan base. Off the court, he is active on social media and has become a brand ambassador for several major products. Kevin is an inspiration for the younger generation, admired for his hard work, dedication, and love for badminton. More than just an athlete, he is a lifestyle icon and a proud symbol of Indonesian sports on the global stage.

Who are The Minions?

"The Minions" is the nickname for the Indonesian men's doubles badminton pair Kevin Sanjaya Sukamuljo and Marcus Fernaldi Gideon. They earned this nickname because of their relatively short height compared to other international players and their fast, energetic, and entertaining style of play, similar to the lively animated characters called Minions from the popular movie franchise.
